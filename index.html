About CountText - Online Character Count Tool

What is CountText?

CountText is a professional, advanced text analysis suite and character counter. It is designed to help copywriters, developers, social media professionals, and SEO experts analyze their texts instantly. The tool offers real-time statistics including character counts, word density, reading ease, and interactive visual previews for major digital platforms.

How is it used?

Simply start typing or paste your content in the main editor. CountText will immediately compute total characters, sentences, paragraphs, and reading times. Use the navigation bar to switch to **Social Previews** to draft platform-specific posts for Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Instagram, and YouTube. Switch to the **SEO Analyzer** to view Google search result snippet previews in desktop and mobile layout sizes.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 6, 2026

Welcome to CountText. Your privacy is paramount to us. This privacy policy explains how we treat your data when you use our website tool.

1. No Server-Side Text Processing

Unlike other counting tools, CountText does not transmit the text you write, paste, or upload to any backend server. All counts, analytics, density checks, readability index calculations, and social previews occur purely client-side within your browser using JavaScript. Your text remains local to your device.

2. Local Storage Usage

To prevent data loss if you accidentally close or reload your browser, CountText saves your active drafts, target keywords, and SEO settings in your browser's local memory (localStorage). This data is stored on your hard drive and can only be accessed by your browser. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser cache or using the "Clear" button in the editor.

3. Advertisements & Third-Party Cookies

We serve non-intrusive advertisements on CountText to monetize the website. Third-party vendors, including Google (via AdSense), use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

4. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
