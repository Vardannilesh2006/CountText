Key Instagram Limits at a Glance

  • Post Captions: Max 2,200 characters.
  • Bio Characters: Max 150 characters.
  • Hashtags per Post: Max 30 hashtags.
  • Accounts Mentions: Max 20 accounts.
  • Instagram Username: Max 30 characters.

The 'More' Button Truncation: 125 Characters

Although Instagram grants you 2,200 characters for post captions, only the first **125 characters** are visible in the user's home feed. The rest is hidden behind a small "...more" link. If your copy does not hook the viewer within these 125 characters, you miss the opportunity to convert them.

Hashtag Limits and Warnings

If you include even one hashtag over the 30-hashtag limit, Instagram will post your image **without any caption at all**. Always count your hashtags. Keeping your hashtags around 5-10 hyper-relevant tags is recommended for the current algorithm rather than stuffing the full 30.
